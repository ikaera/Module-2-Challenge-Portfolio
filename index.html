About


If anyone thinks he knows something, he does not yet know it as he should know it.
This quote from God's Word, the Bible, which I admire, describes the growth mindset very well. For me it means actively seeking learning opportunities.
This way of thinking is vital and helpful to me who desires to be more and more involved in the ever-developing and ever-advancing computer science industry.

I have passion for programming and love learning new skills to make more useful applications and programs for the people to simplify our lives. It is really awesome that computer science combines two wonderful things: mathematics and technology, both of which interest me very very much.
At the end of the day what matters most is that we all can agree with the following: technology will never stop developing and advancing, each advancement only fuelling the further ingenuity and imagination of computer scientists and engineers.
I too want to be part of these significant developments. If it is possible, I want to help a little bit create the technology of the future, not just use it!
